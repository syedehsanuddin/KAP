Some common questions
often asked

Where is KAP clothing manufactured?

KAP clothing is proudly manufactured in Pakistan. Our journey begins with sourcing high-quality fabrics from Faisalabad, followed by precision stitching in Sialkot, and final assembly in the bustling city of Karachi.

Does KAP offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. Wherever you are, you can experience the craftsmanship and style of KAP clothing.

Can I return or exchange my purchase?

We want you to love your KAP clothing. If, for any reason, you are not satisfied, please contact us for Returns and Exchanges policy for detailed information on the process.

How do I care for my KAP garments?

To keep your KAP clothing in pristine condition, we recommend following the care instructions on the garment's label.

How can I contact KAP's customer support?

For any inquiries or assistance, you can reach out to our customer support team through the Contact Us page on our website. We are here to help!

How often does KAP release new collections?

We regularly introduce new collections to keep our offerings fresh and exciting.
